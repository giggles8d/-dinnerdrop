## Keyword Strategy Notes

### Campaign Structure Recommendation
Build 3 tightly-themed ad groups to maximize Quality Score:

**Ad Group 1 — "Meal Planning Apps"** (keywords 1–5)
- Focus: Capturing existing app-seekers already in the funnel
- Match types: Exact + Phrase on #1–2; Broad Match Modified on #3–5
- Landing page: /beta (beta offer front and center)
- Budget allocation: 40% of Google budget

**Ad Group 2 — "AI Meal Planner"** (keywords 6–10)
- Focus: Capturing early-adopter, tech-comfortable users
- Match types: Exact on #6, #8; Phrase on #7, #9–10
- Landing page: /beta (emphasize the AI angle in ad headline)
- Budget allocation: 35% of Google budget

**Ad Group 3 — "Family Dinner Planning"** (keywords 11–20)
- Focus: Busy parents — highest intent, best conversion potential
- Match types: Exact on #11–13; Broad Match Modified on #14–20
- Landing page: /beta (lead with family/time-saving angle)
- Budget allocation: 25% of Google budget

## Negative Keywords (Add at Campaign Level)

Exclude irrelevant traffic to protect budget:
- recipe (people browsing recipes, not seeking an app)
- free recipes
- HelloFresh, Blue Apron, meal kit (meal delivery, not planning)
- diet plan, keto diet, weight loss (different intent)
- restaurant, takeout
- slow cooker recipes, instant pot recipes (recipe browsers)

## Competitor Keyword Opportunities

These competitor brand + "alternative" terms have lower CPC and target users actively seeking a switch:

| Competitor Keyword | Est. Volume | Est. CPC | Notes |
|-------------------|-------------|----------|-------|
| mealime alternative | 500–1K | $1.50 | Mealime is the most-searched alternative |
| plan to eat alternative | 200–500 | $1.30 | Price-sensitive audience |
| eat this much alternative | 200–500 | $1.20 | Nutrition-focused, may not convert |
| meal planning app free trial | 1K–3K | $1.80 | DinnerDrop's 7-day trial is a strong match |

**Note:** Competitor brand bidding (e.g. "Mealime" alone) is more expensive and carries legal risk. Stick to "alternative" and "vs" terms.

---

## Beta Offer Keyword Angle

DinnerDrop's strongest differentiator for paid search is the beta offer:
- "free meal planning app" — high volume but poor quality; add negative keyword "free forever"
- "meal planning app free trial" — HIGH PRIORITY — matches the 7-day trial offer exactly
- Use extensions: Sitelink ("6 months free for beta users"), Callout ("Only 100 spots"), Structured Snippet
