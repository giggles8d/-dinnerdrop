## Full Description (for the product page body)

### The problem

Every Sunday night, the same conversation happens in millions of homes:

"What do you want for dinner this week?"
"I don't know. What do you want?"

Then someone opens Pinterest. Then closes it. Then orders pizza.

The average family spends **45 minutes per week** just deciding what to eat — and that's before grocery shopping, before cooking, before managing picky eaters and dietary restrictions.

### What DinnerDrop does

DinnerDrop is an AI-powered weekly dinner planner built specifically for families.

In one tap, it generates **5 personalized dinner meals for the week** — matched to your family size, dietary needs (gluten-free, vegetarian, nut-free, keto, halal, and more), max cook time, and preferred cooking style.

Then it builds your complete grocery list automatically.

Then it hands the grocery list off to your preferred store — **Instacart, Walmart, Amazon Fresh, or Kroger** — so you go from "I have no idea what we're eating this week" to a full cart ready for checkout in under 2 minutes.

### What makes it different

- **No subscription box.** You pick your own groceries from your own store.
- **No weekly meal kit markup.** Use your loyalty card, your store's sale prices, your pantry.
- **Pantry scan.** Take a photo of your fridge or pantry — the AI removes what you already have from your grocery list automatically.
- **Swap any meal.** Tap the refresh icon on any meal card to swap it out without regenerating everything.
- **Meal history.** Every week's plan is saved. Browse the last 3 months and re-run your family's favorites.
- **Trial-to-paid — no credit card required.** The 7-day free trial starts with a $0 checkout. No card collected until you decide to subscribe.

### Beta offer (first 100 families)

The first 100 families get **6 months completely free** — no credit card, no catch. Just real meal planning while the product is in beta. After 6 months, it's $9/month.

We'll email you 7 days before billing starts, and you can cancel anytime before that.
